Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: JULY 2025

Short version: Resurgex does not collect your files, scan results, or usage data. The application runs entirely on your computer. The only data we receive is your email address (from Paddle at purchase) and a machine identifier used to validate your license activation.

1. Information We Collect

At purchase: When you buy Resurgex through Paddle, Paddle shares your email address with us so we can deliver your license key. Paddle handles all payment information — we never see your card number or billing details.

At license activation: When you activate Resurgex, the application sends an anonymous machine identifier (a hash derived from hardware properties) to our activation server to verify your license is valid and not used on too many devices. No personally identifiable information is included in this request beyond your license key.

Update checks: On startup, Resurgex fetches a version number from our website (resurgexapp.com/version.json) to check for updates. This is a plain HTTP GET request — no identifying information is sent.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Any files or data from your storage devices
  • Scan results or recovery history
  • Usage statistics or telemetry
  • Your name, location, or IP address
  • Any information beyond what is described above

3. How We Use Your Information

Your email address is used solely to deliver your license key after purchase. We do not send marketing emails. We do not sell or share your information with third parties except as required to deliver the service (Paddle for payment processing).

4. Third-Party Services

Paddle: Payments are processed by Paddle (paddle.com/legal/privacy), who acts as the Merchant of Record. Paddle collects and processes your payment and billing information under their own privacy policy.

5. Data Retention

License records (your email and license key) are retained as long as necessary to provide support and validate activations. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us — note that deleting your license record will deactivate your license.

6. Security

License activation requests are transmitted over HTTPS. License keys are cryptographically signed and validated server-side. We do not store payment information.

7. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact support@resurgexapp.com.
